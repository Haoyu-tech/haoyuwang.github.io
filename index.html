About

I am a PhD student in Financial Mathematics at the University of Auckland, supported by a full scholarship. My research lies at the intersection of quantitative finance, machine learning, and stochastic processes — with a particular focus on portfolio optimisation, algorithmic trading strategies, and deep learning applications in financial markets.

Prior to my PhD, I completed an MSc in Financial Mathematics (with First Honours – Distinction) at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University and gained industry experience at S&P Global (IHS Markit) and Volvo Group, working on quantitative research, machine learning modelling, and data analysis.

My work has been published and is under review in journals including Quantitative Finance and Economics, Expert Systems with Applications, Financial Innovation, and Journal of Real Estate Finance and Economics.
